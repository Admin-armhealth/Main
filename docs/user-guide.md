### 1. Create Your Account

2. Enter your clinic/hospital name
3. Fill in your name and email

## Core Features

### Pre-Authorization Requests

Generate AI-powered Letters of Medical Necessity:

1. Navigate to **Pre-Auth** from the sidebar
2. Fill in patient and provider information
3. Upload clinical notes (PDF, image, or paste text)
4. Add CPT/ICD codes
5. Select the insurance payer
6. Click **Generate Document**
7. Review the AI-generated letter
8. Export as PDF or copy to clipboard

### Appeal Letters

When a claim is denied:

1. Navigate to **Appeals** from the sidebar
2. Enter the denial reason
3. Upload supporting clinical documentation
4. Click **Generate Appeal**
5. Review and customize the response
6. Export for submission

## Tips for Best Results

### Clinical Notes

- **More detail = better results**: Include diagnosis, treatment history, and medical necessity
- **Include dates**: Conservative therapy duration, symptom onset
- **Specify procedures**: Use exact CPT codes

### Uploads

- Supported formats: **PDF**, **PNG**, **JPG**
- Scanned documents are OCR-processed automatically
- Maximum file size: 10MB

## Account Management

### Reset Password

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check inbox for reset link
4. Set new password

### Profile Settings

Navigate to **Settings** to:

- Manage team members
